HERE ARE FEW BEST MOVIES TO WATCH WHEN BORED

Top 10 Movies of all time

  1. The Shawshank Redemption (1994) – IMDb: 9.3



    A powerful story of friendship, hope, and survival inside a maximum-security prison.

  2. The Dark Knight (2008) – IMDb: 9.0



    A gripping superhero thriller with Christian Bale and Heath Ledger's iconic performance as the Joker.

  3. Inception (2010) – IMDb: 8.8



    A mind-bending heist film about dreams within dreams, directed by Christopher Nolan.

  4. Fight Club (1999) – IMDb: 8.8



    A dark, satirical take on consumerism and identity, starring Brad Pitt and Edward Norton.

  5. The Matrix (1999) – IMDb: 8.7



    A groundbreaking sci-fi action film that questions reality, with unforgettable action sequences.

  6. Forrest Gump (1994) – IMDb: 8.8



    A heartwarming journey of a man with a big heart, witnessing history through simple eyes.

  7. Schindler’s List (1993) – IMDb: 9.0



    A haunting and emotional true story of a businessman who saves Jewish lives during the Holocaust.

  8. The Godfather (1972) – IMDb: 9.2



    An epic saga of family, power, and crime in this cinematic masterpiece.

  9. Pulp Fiction (1994) – IMDb: 8.9



    Quentin Tarantino’s iconic, non-linear crime thriller full of unforgettable characters and moments.

  10. 12 Angry Men (1957) – IMDb: 9.0



    A compelling courtroom drama about the deliberations of a jury deciding the fate of a young man.
